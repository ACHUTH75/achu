Departments

 

The various academic departments in the institute offers courses in eight branches of engineering at graduation level and six post graduate degree courses. The College also offers part-time degree courses in Civil, Mechanical and Electrical & Electronics Engineering. It is also an approved research centre for Mechanical and Civil Engineering. The college is supported by about 500 imminent staff members, teaching and non-teaching and about 1000 students are admitted every year in various disciplines.
